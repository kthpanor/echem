Equations¶

  • Suppress spin, so \(\psi(\mathbf{r},t)\), not \(\psi(\mathbf{x},t)\).

  • Number equations

    • Prefer not

  • MO indices

    • \(ij...\) (occupied)

    • \(ab...\) (unoccupied)

    • \(pq...\) (general)

    • \(tu...\) (active)

  • Matrices: \(B\) or \(\mathbf{B}\)

    • Prefer bold

  • Greek letters for atomic orbitals

  • Text in equations: \(E_{\mathrm{HF}}\)
